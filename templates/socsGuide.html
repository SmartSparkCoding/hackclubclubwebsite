Joining Guide

Step 1: Sign Up with SOCS

This SOCS Guide explains how to sign up to the Ashford School Hack Club on SOCS!

Step 1.1: Sign Into SOCS

To start the process of signing up for the Ashford School Hack Club, you need to sign into SOCS using the School Portal. A link to the School 'SOCS' portal is below.

SOCS Portal

Once on the link, click Pupil, and sign in with your school email and password.


You are now signed into SOCS! Proceed to the next step!

Step 1.2: Select the Activity & Sign Up!


Once signed in, you will be met with your SOCS dashboard! Simply, click on the Activities tab, then scroll down to Thursday


Our Club is run on Thursdays, from 4:00 PM to 5:30 PM


Once you have selected Thursday, you can then scroll down to the 'Hack Club' activity. You can then click to sign up!


Note:This will not work if you are out of the activity sign-up window. You must contact your form tutor for more help.
